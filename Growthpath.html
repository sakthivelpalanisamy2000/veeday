    Growth Path

    Founded in 2001, Veeday Consultants has grown by leaps and bounds fuelled by the passion to finding the best talent and developing "people potential". We are today, one of the top HR consulting and services firm in Bangalore, India.


    The company has now grown to become a recognized partner to many blue chip organizations as well as smaller businesses, serving clients in the Middle East, India & US. Our reputation and business has been built upon a rare ability to blend experience of blue chip organizations with the detailed attention to individual requirements. In keeping with our philosophy that people are the most important resource of any organization, we have continuously been expanding our team to match our growth.


    We have our Registered office in Bangalore city which is centrally located and easily accessible for our customers. We provide a culture of advancement. We have our Branch offices at Visakhapatnam, and Associate offices at Tirunelveli, Surat, Mumbai. The candidates chosen to work for us are selected not only because they are talented, but also because they are innovators.


    They enjoy developing and/or trying new ideas, like working smarter rather than just harder, prefer an entrepreneurial work environment that's fast paced and dynamic.


    None of Veeday's tremendous success would ever have been possible without our greatest asset: our people. The Veeday team are family rather than just "employees".


    Our work environment is based on a balanced recognition of both individuality and teamwork. We know that all work and no play makes Jack a dull boy. We keep the atmosphere charged with energy through on-going training programs, in-house advancement, half yearly meets and cultural & social gatherings.
